First Job Stories

20 Min
April 20th, 2023
Showcases a guest who shares their unique journey and challenges in landing their initial job, as well as their learning experiences and achievements while working in their chosen field. The podcast aims to provide insights, inspiration, and guidance to individuals who are just starting their careers or considering a career change. The stories are relatable and authentic, and the conversations are insightful and engaging, making it an ideal listen for anyone seeking motivation and practical advice in navigating the professional world.
